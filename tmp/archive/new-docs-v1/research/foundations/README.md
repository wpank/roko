## What Belongs Here

A foundations page covers **theoretical prior art**: science or mathematics developed
outside Roko that Roko's design draws on. It should:

- State the theory clearly enough that a reader unfamiliar with it can understand the
  key claims.
- Identify the specific claims or results that are relevant to Roko.
- Link to perspective pages that develop the "how does this lens illuminate Roko?" question
  in more depth.
- Not duplicate the perspective pages — the foundations page answers "what is the theory?"
  and the perspective pages answer "how do we see Roko through this theory?"
