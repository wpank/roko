# Domain: Formal Methods

Innovations that apply formal verification, model checking, and weakest-precondition calculus to agent planning and learning.

| Slug | Description |
|---|---|
| [dream-verification](../dream-verification.md) | Generate verification conditions (VCs) from REM imagination scenarios; check against six invariant types; turn violations into AntiKnowledge entries rather than undetected risks. |

## Key papers

- Hao, Guan et al. (2024). SafeDreamer: Safe Reinforcement Learning with World Models. ICLR 2024, arXiv:2307.07176.
- Lee et al. (2025). VeriPlan: Integrating Formal Verification and LLMs into End-User Planning. CHI 2025, arXiv:2502.17898.
- Hao, Chen, Zhang & Fan (2024). LLMs Can Solve Real-World Planning Rigorously with Formal Verification Tools. NAACL 2025, arXiv:2404.11891.
- Dijkstra (1976). A Discipline of Programming. Prentice-Hall.
