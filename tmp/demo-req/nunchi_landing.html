03 · THE RUNTIME

Observe. Decide. Enforce. Record.

The control loop the field said couldn't close on agents. Every stage external to the model. Every stage deterministic. Every stage replayable.

01 · OBSERVE

Pulse

ephemeral · structured

Every action emits structured events on the bus — token, latency, tool, gate, decision. Captured at source, not reconstructed after.

02 · DECIDE

Score · Route

cascade · context

Cascade routing chooses model and parameters per task. Knowledge substrate injects context. Reputation gates trust.

03 · ENFORCE

Gate pipeline

11 gates · language-agnostic

Compile, test, diff, lint, security, semantic, coverage, budget, latency, policy, replay. Failures route back. Frontier calls are last resort.

04 · RECORD

Signal

durable · cited

Content-addressed. HDC-fingerprinted. Replayable. Citable cross-org. Becomes context for the next agent on the next task.

Pulse
The ephemeral primitive.
Every nunchi.audit(), every tool call, every gate decision is a Pulse. They live on the bus, not on disk — the runtime decides which become Signals.
Signal
The durable primitive.
Content-addressed events written to the substrate. HDC-fingerprinted. Reputable. The history that lets the next agent skip the failures of the last.
Cell
The execution primitive.
An agent execution context bound to an ERC-8004 identity. Cells are scoped, sandboxed, and replayable. The unit billed by the cloud, the unit slashable on the chain.

HDC
PRECOMPILE 0xA01
Hyperdimensional similarity over 10,240-bit binary vectors. Native EVM precompile, ~400 gas top-K. The substrate that makes cross-org knowledge queryable on-chain at a price agents can pay.
ERC-8004
AGENT IDENTITY
Transferable on-chain identity with 7-domain reputation EMA — coding, security, research, chain, knowledge, ops, strategy. Slashable on policy violation. Queryable cross-org without disclosure.
ZK-HDC
CIRCOM · GROTH16
Hamming distance proofs over committed hypervectors. Sub-second proving on a laptop. ~250K gas to verify. Lets one agent prove it knows something without revealing what.
