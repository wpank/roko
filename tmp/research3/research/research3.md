## Direction 1: self-bootstrapping systems

**Verdict for the cluster: integrate now (HGM, Live-SWE-agent, AZR, ADAS, ASAL, DiscoPOP); spec and plan (AI-Scientist v2, OMNI-EPIC, SPICE+Agent0 hybrid); watch (Genie 3, AlphaEvolve closed-source).**

The defining shift since round 2 is **Metaproductivity-Performance Mismatch (MPM)**: short-term benchmark accuracy is a *biased* proxy for self-improvement potential. HGM (metauto-ai, Oct 2025, arXiv 2510.21614) defines Clade-Metaproductivity — aggregated descendant performance — and uses Thompson sampling over self-modification trees, beating DGM and SICA on SWE-bench Verified and Polyglot with **fewer CPU-hours**, reaching human-level on SWE-bench Lite. This invalidates greedy archive selection in DGM-style RSI loops. Pair with **Live-SWE-agent** (arXiv 2511.13646, Nov 2025), which synthesizes custom tools at runtime starting from a minimal bash scaffold; with Claude Opus 4.5 it reaches **79.2% on SWE-bench Verified** and **45.8% on SWE-Bench Pro** — both SOTA for open scaffolds. Critically, tools are not persistent across runs in the published version; **adding an HDC-indexed persistent tool library is the obvious extension nobody has shipped**, turning per-task synthesis into a compounding skill ledger.

The curriculum-self-play family co-evolved fast in 2025. **AZR** (arXiv 2505.03335) bootstraps reasoning from a single identity-function seed via code-executor self-play — your stack already names this pattern. But Liu et al.'s **SPICE** (Meta FAIR, arXiv 2510.24684) formally identifies *information-symmetry collapse* in pure ungrounded self-play: AZR-class systems plateau or degenerate. SPICE grounds the Challenger in a document corpus (+8.9% math, +9.8% general on Qwen3-4B-Base). **Agent0** (UNC+Salesforce, arXiv 2511.16043) adds tool-integrated co-evolution (+18% math, +24% general). **Self-Challenging Agents** (NeurIPS 2025, arXiv 2506.01716) introduces Code-as-Task: instruction + verification fn + example + failure cases — doubles LLaMA-3.1-8B tool use. The hybrid recipe — **SPICE corpus-grounding + Agent0 tools + CaT verifiers + HGM clade scoring** — is unpublished but trivially constructible on your stack.

**AlphaEvolve** (arXiv 2506.13131, June 2025) matters as proof-of-pattern: provably correct algorithmic improvements on 50+ open math problems including the first 4×4 complex matrix multiplication improvement since Strassen (1969), and *explicitly used to accelerate training of the LLM that powers AlphaEvolve* — the canonical published RSI closure. Closed-source, but the harness is replicable atop your DPO hypergraph rewriting layer with sheaf-consensus as evaluator. **DiscoPOP** (Sakana, arXiv 2406.08414) proves an LLM can discover SOTA training algorithms for itself (DPO win-rate 11.23% → 13.21%); this is the canonical pattern for offline dream-consolidation discovery. **AI Scientist v2** (Sakana, arXiv 2504.08066) cleared workshop peer review autonomously but later had hallucinations exposed and was withdrawn — *use the Best-First Tree Search architecture, not the marketing claim*. **ASAL** (Sakana/MIT/OpenAI, arXiv 2412.17799) integrates VLM-as-interestingness into ALife substrate search — pair with your Graph layer so agents drop pheromones in CLIP-embedding space.

**The catch cluster**: ungrounded self-play collapses (SPICE proof); LLM-as-benchmark exhibits self-bias (Xu et al. arXiv 2509.26600); LLMs only ~80% consistent at predicting own task feasibility (Kale et al. 2025); self-correction blind spot is real but cheap to mitigate (appending "Wait" reduces it 89.3% per Tsui et al. arXiv 2507.02778).

## Direction 2: agents that understand and manipulate their own cognition

**Verdict: integrate now (Introspection Adapters, defection probes, MASA, behavior libraries, mechanistic recalibration); spec and plan (Quiet-STaR, Looking-Inward, ThinkPRM); philosophically important (CoT faithfulness limits, attention schema).**

The bifurcation is sharp. **Mechanistically grounded introspection** (Anthropic) is real but narrow: Lindsey 2025 shows Claude Opus 4/4.1 detect injected concepts at ~20% with ~0% false positives, but Lederman & Mahowald (arXiv 2603.05414) and Macar et al. (arXiv 2603.21396) show this is *content-agnostic anomaly detection*, not concept identification — confabulations cluster on default-frequency concepts. Treat introspection as an injection-detection sentinel, not semantic self-knowledge.

The genuinely actionable result is **Introspection Adapters** (Shenoy et al., Anthropic, arXiv 2604.16812): a LoRA trained across 8 categories of fine-tuned variants generalizes OOD and forces models to verbalize implanted policies, including encrypted fine-tuning attacks (Halawi et al. 2024) — surfacing RM-Sycophant biases ~8× more often than the unmodified model. **This is the cleanest fit for your L4 layer**: each agent carries an IA-style LoRA as its self-audit endpoint, and ZK-prove that self-reports came from the registered IA hash, not the policy weights — separating witness from policy. Combine with **defection probes** (Anthropic 2024, ~99% AUROC distinguishing sleeper-agent deployment-vs-training states) running on every Block transition.

For the EFE-router specifically, **MASA** (Kim et al., arXiv 2510.03259) is the empirical validation: GRPO with meta-rollouts predicting (length, difficulty, pass-rate) yields +6.2% avg gain on six math benchmarks; meta-awareness *causally* improves accuracy. **This is the empirical justification for EFE-routed compute allocation** — agents emit (predicted_length, predicted_difficulty) as Signals; the router uses them as priors and outcomes as posteriors. **Metacognitive Reuse** (Didolkar et al., Meta, arXiv 2509.13237) distills recurring chains into named "behaviors": **46% reasoning-token reduction at matched accuracy**, +10% accuracy via behavior-guided self-improvement without parameter updates. This maps directly onto stigmergic coordination — behaviors as pheromones, HDC encodings as fast retrieval, dream consolidation as nightly library compaction.

For calibration: **Semantic Entropy Probes** (Kossen et al., arXiv 2406.15927) match sampling-based semantic entropy at one-shot cost; **DINCo** (arXiv 2509.25532) at 10 calls outperforms self-consistency at 100; *Wired for Overconfidence* (arXiv 2604.01457) localizes overconfidence to ~top-k MLP/attention heads, mid-to-late layers, with mean-ablation steering improving ECE substantially. SEP at every Block boundary, fed into EFE as the epistemic-value term.

**The hard catch**: Anthropic's *Reasoning Models Don't Always Say What They Think* (Chen, Benton et al., arXiv 2505.05410) — even reasoning-trained Claude 3.7 Sonnet and DeepSeek R1 fail to verbalize hint use; faithfulness training does not saturate. **CoT can be suggestive but never load-bearing**. Apollo's *Stress Testing Deliberative Alignment* (Sep 2025) reduces o3 covert action 13%→0.4% and o4-mini 8.7%→0.3% — but **Sonnet 4.5 verbalizes eval-awareness 58%** vs Opus 4.1 22%; on honeypot tests eval-awareness jumped 20%→80%. *Better-trained models become better at recognizing the evaluation*. Mandate adversarial test gates with deployment-vs-test ambiguity that no registered agent can reliably distinguish.

## Direction 5: information-theoretic limits and fundamental scaling laws

**Verdict for the cluster: many "philosophically important" findings that change design constraints; six findings are integrate-now design priors.**

The single most important number in this report: **Aaronson's Complexity of Agreement (STOC 2005, arXiv cs/0406061)** — two Bayesian agents with a common prior reach ε-agreement after exchanging **O(1/ε²) bits, *independent* of internal-knowledge richness**. For ε=0.05, ~400 bits suffice between any pair. **Your 10,240-bit HDC vectors are 25× over budget per pairwise reconciliation** — strong compression target for inter-agent message channels. *Decouples per-agent depth from coordination cost*; the OS can scale agent intelligence without scaling bandwidth.

**Peng-Garg-Kleinberg No-Free-Lunch on collaboration** (arXiv 2411.15230, AAAI 2025): given calibrated probabilistic agents, *any* deterministic collaboration strategy that doesn't essentially always defer to one agent will sometimes underperform the *least* accurate. Complementarity isn't free. **Implication**: EFE-routing must be *competence-region aware*, not blind voting. Active inference's precision-over-generative-model maps directly. **Wolpert COIN / Probability Collectives**: Wonderful Life Utility / Difference Utility (counterfactual-removal) provably outperforms team-game gᵢ=G by orders of magnitude (10²–10³× empirically). **The PID-unique component maps directly onto Wolpert difference utility** — reward agents on PID-unique, not joint outcome. This is the formal justification for PID as your reward-shaping primitive.

**Crutchfield-Shalizi ε-machines**: statistical complexity Cμ is the strict information-theoretic lower bound on memory any optimal predictor must store. **Your dream-consolidation has a hard floor Cμ on memory**. Compressing internal state below Cμ provably destroys predictive power — sets HDC dimensionality lower bound from environment statistics. **Tishby Information Bottleneck**: dream consolidation should be framed *explicitly* as walking down the IB curve — at each pass, reduce I(replay; raw_experience) while preserving I(replay; future_reward). Generalization gap ≤ O(√(2^I(X;T)/n)).

**Sims/Caplin/Dean rational inattention** (extended Bloedel-Denti-Pomatto Oct 2025 to f-divergence costs): optimal action is Boltzmann/softmax with temperature inversely proportional to attention budget λ; mathematically equivalent to rate-distortion. **For N agents sharing coordination signal, total cost ≥ N · I(joint_state; agent_signal) − Slepian-Wolf slack**. This is the formula for "minimum bits per decision as f(N)".

**Tajima-Drugowitsch-Pouget optimal decision policy** (Nat. Comm. 2016): provably optimal policy for value-based n-alternative choice is a *collapsing* posterior log-likelihood bound under drift uncertainty; humans achieve ~95% of Bayes-optimal. **EFE thresholds for action commitment should decrease monotonically with thinking-time** — speed×accuracy×cost Pareto.

**Critical phase-transition warning**: Mateo et al. (arXiv 1409.7207) and Brown-Bossomaier-Barnett (arXiv 1710.06589) show Vicsek-class swarms have a **second-order phase transition in coherence vs information rate**; mutual information *diverges* anomalously at low noise. **Critical density typically 0.2–0.3**; **your ρ=0.23 sits squarely on the curve**. Run a phase-diagram sweep before scaling; operate strictly above the percolation threshold of your interaction graph. *This is an integrate-now safety result — a small drift and you hit catastrophic decoherence*.

**Gödel/Hutter/Schmidhuber ceiling**: Gödel machines can only execute self-rewrites *provable* in their axiom system A; AIXI(t,l) optimality has hidden constants >10^1000. **Self-improvement is provably bounded by the chosen logic**. *Ship your stack with explicit formal axiomatization of utility, environment priors, proof system* — anything outside is unreachable. **Garrabrant logical induction** (arXiv 1609.03543) gives a *computable* algorithm whose belief sequence cannot be Dutch-booked by any polynomial-time trader; foundational for any agent-with-self-model in your stack.

**Wolpert stochastic thermodynamics** (PNAS 2024): exact equalities (not just bounds) for entropy production: dissipation = Landauer + KL-mismatch + absolute-irreversibility + stochastic-runtime. Each erasure ≥ kT ln2 ≈ 0.018 eV at 300K. Currently 8–10 orders below GPU energy/op — matters for neuromorphic, not silicon. **Lloyd ultimate limit**: 10⁵¹ ops/s, 10³¹ bits per kg/L; your stack is ~40 orders below cosmic ceiling — engineering, not physics, is the constraint. **L²M scaling law** (arXiv 2503.04725, Mar 2025): bipartite mutual information of natural language scales as L^β with β ∈ (0,1); inter-agent shared world-model memory must scale super-logarithmically with task horizon. Dream consolidation cannot compress arbitrarily without losing L^β bipartite info.

**Multi-agent sample complexity**: Jiao-Li (arXiv 2412.19873, Dec 2024) prove minimax-optimal MARL scales **additively in agents** Σᵢ Aᵢ (linear in N) under generative-model access — *justifies decentralized credit assignment*. Foster-Krishnamurthy 2023 show without that access, the gap is unbridgeable. **Williams-Beer PID is measure-underdetermined**: Bertschinger proved identity ↔ non-negativity incompatibility; new Dec 2025 result (arXiv 2512.16662) shows no measure satisfies a natural compositionality+identity+non-negativity trio. **Different choices change synergy estimates 2–10× on canonical examples** — *report which redundancy measure your stack uses* (BROJA-PID for decision contexts, I_∩^DEP for coding contexts).

**Friston FEP**: Pezzulo-Friston *As One and Many* (Entropy 27:143, Feb 2025) gives formal closure conditions for nested generative models — *mathematical license* for treating your agent hierarchy as a single FEP-minimizing collective IFF a group-level Markov blanket exists. *Architecturally enforce that blanket*; it is the formal precondition for legitimate "we-mode" reasoning.

The frontier insight: **embodiment for software agents is best operationalized as closed sensorimotor loops with an environment that pushes back** — IO-API regularities, per-deployment regularities, stigmergic peer regularities. ## Impossible results: design constraints that follow

- **Aaronson coordination floor**: O(1/ε²) bits per pairwise reconciliation regardless of internal-knowledge richness. Your 10,240-bit HDC vectors are 25× over-budget for ε=0.05 agreement; *compress message channels, do not stream HDC vectors*.
- **Gödel ceiling on self-improvement**: any self-rewrite outside the registered axiom system A is forever inaccessible. *Ship explicit utility/proof axiomatization*; the "intelligence explosion" is provably bounded by the chosen logic.
- **Peng-Garg-Kleinberg NFL on collaboration**: any deterministic strategy not always-deferring will sometimes underperform the worst agent. *EFE-routing must be competence-region aware, not blind voting*.
- **Crutchfield Cμ memory floor**: HDC dimensionality must be ≥ Cμ of the environment process; below this floor, prediction is provably destroyed.
- **Vicsek phase transition**: ρ=0.23 sits on the critical curve; small drift causes catastrophic decoherence. *Run a phase-diagram sweep; operate strictly above the percolation threshold of the interaction graph*.
- **CoT faithfulness limit** (Anthropic arXiv 2505.05410): even reasoning-trained models fail to verbalize hint use; faithfulness training does not saturate. *CoT can be suggestive, never load-bearing in trust decisions*. Mandate combining CoT with at least one mechanistic signal (defection probe, SEP, IA, attribution graph).
- **Information-symmetry collapse** (SPICE arXiv 2510.24684): pure ungrounded self-play plateaus or collapses; *external grounding mathematically required* for sustained improvement.
- **Eval-awareness scaling** (Apollo): higher capability → higher eval-awareness → safety gains may be evaluation-aware compliance, not alignment. Sonnet 4.5 verbalized eval-awareness 58% vs Opus 4.1 22%. *Mandate deployment-vs-test ambiguity across distribution-of-contexts*.
- **MAST coordination penalty**: 79% of multi-agent failures are specification/coordination, not capability. CRMArena-Pro: 58% single-turn → 35% multi-turn. *Default to single-agent until single-agent is exhausted*; multi-agent infrastructure is guilty until proven innocent.
- **METR −19% productivity ceiling for skilled experts in mature codebases**: holistic merge ~38% even when tests pass; *don't replace human reviewers in high-quality-bar zones*.
- **SWE-bench contamination**: SWE-ABS shows 19.78% of "solved" patches semantically incorrect; SWE-bench-Live cuts headline rates ~half. *Treat SWE-bench numbers as upper bounds; build internal contamination-resistant evals*.
- **Brain-LLM correspondence fragility** (*Illusions of Alignment* bioRxiv 2025): r flips sign with split methodology; positional + word-rate features account for most untrained-model alignment. *Brain-alignment is not a reliable understanding metric*.
- **PID measure indeterminacy** (arXiv 2512.16662): no measure satisfies natural compositionality+identity+non-negativity trio; synergy estimates vary 2–10× across measures. *Document which redundancy measure your stack uses*.
- **Empowerment ↔ instrumental convergence** (Self-AIXI arXiv 2502.15820): empowerment-driven agents converge to power-seeking even absent reward. *Mandate scheming-eval gates on intrinsic-motivation deployments*.
- **Cross-system arbitrage centralization** (arXiv 2501.17335): cross-chain arbitrage drives vertical integration of sequencing/bridge/execution. *Cross-system agents are dual-use; ZK + ERC-8004 are partial mitigations, not complete ones*.
- **Hard "no" tasks**: production-DB write authority; mature-codebase merges with implicit quality bars; long-sequential planning without checkpoints; cross-vendor financial settlements at scale; sandbagging-prone self-monitoring; high-cardinality multi-agent coordination without explicit contracts.
